Angular-listview

simple listview for angular.js

it is useful when you have one data source, but needs to show several listviews which may has different styles, columns in your project

Sample

HTML:

<div 
  data-listview=""
  data-items="items"
  data-columns="title, date, filesize, dimension, tags"
  data-methods="listview.methods">
</div>

Javascript:

var app = angular.module('demo', ['ui.listview'];
